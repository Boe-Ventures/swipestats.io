| 15 | 0.9% |
| 16-20 | 0.7% |

**Notes:**
- These are averages across industries. Dating/analytics content may vary.
- Featured snippets, ads, and SERP features can significantly reduce organic CTR.
- Use a 30% underperformance threshold (actual < expected * 0.7) to flag opportunities.
- Pages with very high impressions but position 1-3 and low CTR likely have SERP feature competition.

**Source:** Aggregated from multiple CTR studies (Advanced Web Ranking, Backlinko, Sistrix).
