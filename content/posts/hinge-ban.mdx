<TLDR>

### If Hinge Banned Your Account

- Open Hinge and use the **Appeal** option shown on the ban screen.
- Explain why you believe the decision was incorrect and include concise, relevant evidence.
- Hinge may require a government-issued ID during or after the appeal.
- Hinge says appeals must use the designated in-app process; social messages and emails to employees are not processed as appeals.
- Repeated outreach can change your position in the review queue and increase the wait.
- Do not create new numbers, devices, accounts, photos, or payment identities to evade the ban.

</TLDR>

## Why Hinge Bans Accounts

Hinge says content can be removed and accounts can be permanently banned for violating its Terms of Service or Community Guidelines. The guidelines cover age, authenticity, harassment, hate, safety, illegal activity, commercial behavior, and other prohibited conduct.

A ban notice may not disclose every moderation signal because reports and safety investigations can involve other members' privacy. That makes a focused appeal more useful than trying to reverse-engineer the reporter.

## How to Appeal a Hinge Ban

1. Open the Hinge app.
2. Read the ban screen and any details specific to the account.
3. Tap **Appeal**.
4. Explain why you believe the account did not violate the relevant rule.
5. Attach or provide the requested verification.
6. Submit once and wait for the confirmation.

Hinge says the safety team will follow up by email. Use an address you can access and check spam or filtered folders.

## What to Put in the Appeal

Keep it factual:

- identify the rule or behavior mentioned in the notice;
- state what happened in chronological order;
- acknowledge any part you understand could have been interpreted differently;
- attach screenshots or receipts only when they directly support the account;
- ask for review rather than demanding information about another member.

Avoid threats, insults, repeated submissions, invented explanations, or contacting individual Hinge and Match Group employees.

## When Hinge Requests ID

Hinge may require a valid government-issued ID before an appeal can be submitted or during the investigation. If the in-app flow requires ID and you cannot provide it, Hinge directs members to its request form with **Submit an Appeal** and an ID-verification issue in the subject.

Review Hinge's privacy information before submitting sensitive documents and use only the official Hinge flow linked from the app or help center.

## How Long Does a Hinge Appeal Take?

Hinge does not promise one universal review time on its current appeal page. The time can depend on volume, verification, and complexity.

Hinge specifically warns that additional contact after submission may alter queue position and increase processing time. One complete appeal is better than repeated follow-ups through multiple channels.

## Can You Make a New Hinge Account After a Ban?

Do not attempt to bypass enforcement with a new phone number, email, device account, payment method, photo set, or network identity. That can violate platform rules and interfere with safety systems.

The appropriate path is the official appeal. If it is denied, use only the options Hinge provides in the decision or support documentation.

## Protect Your Data and Billing

Banned accounts can still request a data export from the Ban Notification screen under the Legal section, according to Hinge's data-request help page.

Also verify any subscription in Apple, Google Play, or Hinge/Stripe. Billing management depends on the original payment route; do not assume a ban or app deletion canceled every renewal.

## FAQ

### Can I appeal a Hinge ban?

Hinge says some banned accounts can submit an appeal through the in-app ban screen.

### Can I email Hinge to appeal?

Hinge says appeal submissions must use the designated in-app flow. Email can be used for an ongoing case or a specific ID-verification problem when directed, but it is not the initial appeal route.

### Will Hinge ask for ID?

It may. Some appeals require government-issued ID before submission or during review.

### Can I find out who reported me?

Hinge keeps reports confidential and does not disclose other members' account details. Focus the appeal on your own conduct and evidence.

### How do I bypass a Hinge ban?

Do not. Use the official appeal and support process; this guide does not provide identity or device evasion instructions.
