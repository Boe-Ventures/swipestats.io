<TLDR>

### The Answer

Hinge says it **does not automatically update your location** each time you log in. If you move or travel, update the location manually or use the compass with device-location permission.

</TLDR>

## How Hinge Location Works

Hinge lets you set the dating location with a map. During onboarding or later edits, you can:

- use the compass to move the pin to the device's current location;
- search for a city, neighborhood, address, or ZIP code;
- move the map pin manually;
- choose a broader or more specific location label.

Hinge says it displays the selected neighborhood name rather than an exact address. Google Maps provides the location names.

## How to Change Your Hinge Location

From the profile:

1. Tap the Photo icon.
2. Tap the Pencil icon.
3. Under **My Vitals**, tap **Location**.
4. Use the compass, map, or search field.
5. Press the back arrow to save.

You can also open Preferences from Discover and change **My Neighborhood**.

## Does Hinge Track You in the Background?

Hinge's current help page says it does not automatically update the profile location by registering and recording location each time you log in.

If you tap the compass, the app requests device-level location permission to move the pin. That is a user-triggered update. Review iOS or Android permissions if you no longer want Hinge to access current location for that action.

## Location vs. Hometown

Location represents where someone is currently dating and appears with a compass icon. Hometown is a separate profile field indicating where the person is from.

Changing one does not automatically change the other.

## Distance and Dealbreakers

Maximum Distance controls the radius used for Discovery. Marking it as a Dealbreaker means Hinge should only show people whose selected location falls inside the radius.

Hinge calculates radial distance, not driving or transit time. A short radius can still cross water, borders, or difficult travel routes.

## Using Hinge While Traveling

Update the location to the destination when you genuinely plan to be there. Hinge availability differs by country, and new accounts can only set an initial location in supported countries.

Do not use false locations to mislead matches or bypass platform availability. Be clear in the profile or conversation about travel dates and current location.

## FAQ

### Does Hinge change location when you travel?

No. Hinge says you must update it manually or use the compass.

### Can I set a different city before a trip?

Yes, in supported locations. Be transparent about when you will be there.

### Does Hinge show my exact address?

Hinge says it displays the selected neighborhood name, not the exact address.

### Why am I seeing people outside my preferred distance?

Check whether Maximum Distance is marked as a Dealbreaker. Also remember that Hinge uses radial distance rather than travel time.
