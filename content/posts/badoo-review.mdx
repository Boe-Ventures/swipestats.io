## Frequently Asked Questions About Badoo

### Is Badoo safe?

Sure, if you're trying not to get stabbed. No promises though.

While Badoo has implemented safety features like photo verification, the onus is still on you to stay vigilant. Don't share personal information too quickly, always meet in public places for the first few dates, and trust your gut. If something seems too good to be true, it probably is.

### Can you use Badoo without Facebook?

Yes, you can sign up using your email or phone number. No need to link your Facebook account if you don't want to. This is great for those who prefer to keep their dating life separate from their social media presence.

### How do I delete my Badoo account?

To delete your account, go to Settings > Account > Delete Account. Follow the prompts, and your account will be permanently deleted. Remember, this action is irreversible, so make sure you're certain before pulling the trigger.

### Is Badoo good for serious relationships?

While it's possible to find a serious relationship on Badoo (after all, with 500 million users, anything is possible), it's primarily geared towards casual dating and friendships.

If you're looking for something more serious, you might want to check out our [review of the best dating apps](/blog/best-dating-apps) for alternatives that cater more to long-term relationships.

### How does Badoo compare to other dating apps?

Badoo offers a more global and diverse user base compared to apps like Tinder or Bumble. It's less curated than Hinge, focusing more on the quantity of matches rather than quality.

Think of it as the Walmart of dating apps ‚Äì you can find almost anything there, but you'll need to do some digging to find the good stuff.

### Can I change my location on Badoo?

Yes, Badoo allows you to change your location, which is great for planning ahead if you're traveling or if you want to explore dating options in different areas. However, use this feature responsibly ‚Äì nobody likes being catfished by someone pretending to be local.

### Does Badoo have read receipts?

Badoo does not have read receipts in its free version. This feature is available in the premium version, allowing you to see when your messages have been read. It's a double-edged sword ‚Äì it can provide clarity but also lead to overthinking.

### Can I hide my profile on Badoo?

Yes, Badoo offers an "invisible mode" as part of its premium features. This allows you to browse without your profile being visible to others. It's great for those who want to be more discreet or take a break from active matching without deleting their account.
