<TLDR>

### The Answer

Hinge gives members **one free Rose per week**. Free Roses do not accumulate: if you leave one unused, the next weekly refresh still leaves you with one free Rose, not two. Purchased Roses remain available until you use them.

</TLDR>

## Does the Free Rose Reset at a Specific Time?

Hinge's current Rose help article states the weekly rule but does not promise one universal weekday or clock time for every account. Check the Rose balance shown in your Profile Hub or when sending a Like for the account-specific state.

Avoid relying on an old article that names a fixed Sunday time. Product timing can vary with account creation, market, and app changes, and the official current page is the better source.

## Do Unused Free Roses Roll Over?

No. Hinge explicitly says free Roses do not accumulate.

| Before refresh | After refresh |
| --- | --- |
| 0 free Roses | 1 free Rose |
| 1 unused free Rose | 1 free Rose |

This does not require you to spend the Rose on a poor fit. Letting it expire is better than sending a high-priority signal to someone you would not actually want to meet.

## Do Purchased Roses Expire?

Hinge says purchased Roses remain available until used. They are therefore separate from the weekly free allowance.

Your available Rose balance appears in the Profile Hub and can also appear when you send an outgoing Like.

## Where Can You Send a Rose?

You can send a Rose from Discover or Standouts.

- In Discover, choose a photo or prompt, add an optional comment, and select the Rose instead of a normal Like.
- In Standouts, Roses are the available Like action.

The Rose moves the sender to the top of the recipient's Likes You screen. It does not guarantee that the recipient will accept.

## Should You Use the Rose Before It Resets?

Only when a profile genuinely fits. A free item expiring does not create value by itself.

If you send one, choose the specific profile element that caught your attention and add a short comment when you have a real question or reaction. Avoid a generic line that could accompany any profile.

## FAQ

### How many free Roses do you get?

One per week.

### Can I save free Roses for later?

You can leave the current Rose unused, but it will not stack with the next weekly Rose.

### Do Roses guarantee a match?

No. They receive prominent placement in Likes You; the recipient still decides.

### Do bought Roses disappear every week?

No. Hinge says purchased Roses remain until used.
