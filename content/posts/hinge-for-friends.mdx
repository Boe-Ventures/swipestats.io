<TLDR>

### The Short Answer

Hinge does not document a friendship mode. It presents the product as a dating app for intimate, in-person connection. If you want platonic friends, use a service or local group where everyone opted into that intent.

</TLDR>

## Is There a Hinge Friend Mode?

No current Hinge help page describes a friend toggle, platonic feed, or separate friendship profile. Discover, Likes, Matches, and We Met are all documented around dating.

That does not mean a friendship can never emerge from a date. It means people reviewing a Hinge profile reasonably expect romantic or dating intent unless told otherwise.

## Why Using a Dating Feed for Friends Creates Friction

The problem is not that wanting friends is unusual. The problem is mismatched consent and expectations.

If one person sends a Like expecting a date and the other is using the same interaction to recruit a hiking buddy, both spend time under different assumptions. A bio line saying “friends only” is clearer than hiding the intent, but it still does not turn the rest of the product into a friendship pool.

Hinge's community guidance emphasizes authentic intentions and says the service is for dating rather than promotion, commerce, or gaming the system.

## Better Friendship-Specific Options

### Bumble For Friends

Bumble For Friends is a dedicated friend-finding app and also shares profiles with BFF mode in the Bumble app under its current setup. Both people swipe for platonic connection, then chat after a mutual choice.

The clear intent is the main advantage: nobody needs to decode whether “let's hang out” means a date.

### Interest and activity groups

Look for recurring groups around an activity you would attend even if you met nobody immediately:

- running, climbing, or team sports;
- book clubs or language exchanges;
- volunteering;
- board games and maker spaces;
- professional or alumni communities;
- classes with repeated sessions.

Repeated contact gives a friendship more opportunity to form than a single high-pressure meetup.

### Existing weak ties

Invite a colleague, neighbor, or friend-of-a-friend to a specific low-stakes plan. “Want to get coffee sometime?” is easy to defer. “A few of us are trying the Sunday market at 11—want to join?” is easier to answer.

## If You Already Matched on Hinge

Do not retroactively announce “friends only” after flirting or planning a date. Say clearly that your intent has changed, apologize for the mismatch, and let the other person decide whether they want platonic contact.

For example:

> I want to be transparent: I am not in a place to date, and I should have clarified that before matching. No pressure to stay in touch.

Do not continue searching for dates while telling one specific match they have been moved into a friendship category without explanation.

## Can Couples Use One Hinge Profile to Find Friends?

Hinge's rules say each account must belong to one dater. A joint profile representing two people conflicts with that account model and can create confusion about consent and intent.

Use an event, group, or friendship platform designed for group connections instead.

## Safety Still Applies to Friend Meetups

Meet in public, control your own transportation, tell someone the plan, and do not send money or financial details. A platonic label does not remove the risks of meeting a stranger from an app.

## FAQ

### Can I write “friends only” in my Hinge bio?

You can state your intent, but Hinge does not provide a platonic mode and the surrounding product remains a dating surface.

### Will Hinge ban me for looking for friends?

The public help pages do not publish an automatic outcome for that phrase. They do require authentic use and one dater per account. Use the service for its documented dating purpose.

### Is Bumble For Friends separate from Bumble Date?

Bumble offers a dedicated friend-finding app and BFF mode. Its current privacy page says profiles can appear across those two friendship surfaces.

### What if a Hinge date becomes a friend?

That is a mutual relationship decision, not the same as entering the dating feed with hidden platonic intent.
