<TLDR>

### Tinder Boost in 30 Seconds

- Boost makes the profile one of the top profiles in the local area for 30 minutes.
- Tinder advertises up to 10 times more profile views during the Boost.
- More views do not guarantee Likes, matches, conversations, or dates.
- Gold and Platinum may include a monthly Boost, but plan terms vary because Tinder runs tests.
- Check the exact price and included benefits shown on your own purchase screen.

</TLDR>

## What Is Tinder Boost?

Boost is a paid distribution feature. For 30 minutes, Tinder places the profile among the top profiles in the current area. Tinder says a user may receive up to 10 times more profile views while boosted.

“Up to” is not an individual guarantee. The outcome depends on how many eligible people are active nearby, their Discovery settings, and whether the profile appeals to them.

Boost changes exposure, not preference. It cannot make an incompatible age range, distance, intention, or profile become attractive to someone who would otherwise choose Nope.

## How to Activate a Boost

Open Tinder and tap the purple lightning-bolt icon on the main screen. Once activated, the 30-minute period begins.

Tinder also allows users to buy Boosts from the app or web purchase area:

1. Open the profile.
2. Open Settings.
3. Select the Boost option.
4. Review the current price and terms before confirming.

Do not rely on a price quoted in an article. Tinder's offers vary by account, market, platform, bundle, and test.

## Does Gold or Platinum Include a Boost?

Tinder's current subscription page says eligible Gold and Platinum plans may include one Boost per month. There are important caveats:

- weekly subscriptions do not include the monthly Boost;
- some Gold plans of one month or longer may not include one because of testing;
- an included monthly Boost expires if unused.

The subscription screen shown to your account controls. Verify that a Boost appears in the included-benefits list before subscribing for it.

## When Should You Use It?

Tinder does not publish one universal “best hour.” A reasonable test is a time when:

- you can remain available to review matches and reply;
- people in the target location are likely to be awake and using the app;
- your location and Discovery settings are already correct;
- the profile has been reviewed recently.

Run the test at a time relevant to your actual dating market. A Sunday-evening rule from another city or age group is not evidence that the same window is best for you.

## Is Tinder Boost Worth It?

Treat it as a distribution experiment. Before paying, define what success means:

- more Likes during or shortly after the Boost;
- more matches from those Likes;
- or enough useful conversations to justify the cost.

One Boost does not create a controlled experiment because the active audience changes minute by minute. Still, writing down the starting state and observed outcome is better than judging from the animation alone.

Boost is a weak purchase when the profile has unresolved basics: an unclear lead photo, outdated images, empty bio, incorrect location, or mismatched intentions. Fixing those affects every future impression, while Boost rents a short window of additional impressions.

## What SwipeStats Data Can and Cannot Tell You

A Tinder export can show historical Likes, matches, and activity present in the file. It cannot identify which unseen users viewed the profile during a Boost, nor prove why an individual chose Like or Nope.

Do not label a Boost “successful” from extra swipe activity alone. The most useful downstream signals available in the export are matches and your own conversation activity, with the limitation that incoming messages are not included.

## FAQ

### How long does Tinder Boost last?

Thirty minutes, according to Tinder's current help page.

### Does Boost guarantee 10 times more matches?

No. Tinder advertises up to 10 times more profile views, not matches.

### Can you buy Boost without a subscription?

Yes. Tinder says Boosts can be purchased separately in the app or on Tinder.com.

### Do monthly Boosts roll over?

Tinder says an included monthly Boost expires if it is not used.
