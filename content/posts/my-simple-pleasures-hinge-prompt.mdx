### Small Moments

- Finding the perfect song for your current mood
- When someone texts you back immediately
- That moment right before you fall asleep

- When your phone battery lasts all day

### Sensory Pleasures

- The sound of rain on a window
- Freshly cut grass
- A warm shower after being cold
- The smell of old books
- Soft blankets and good lighting

## What Makes a Good Answer?

1. **Specificity** - "Coffee" is boring. "That first sip of perfectly brewed coffee on a quiet morning" is interesting.
2. **Personality** - Show who you are, not who you think you should be.
3. **Variety** - Mix different types of pleasures to show depth.

<CalloutBox type="success">
  **Pro Tip:** Think about what actually makes you happy in your daily life.
  Those small moments of joy are what people connect with.
</CalloutBox>
