## How NOT to Answer the 'I Go Crazy For' Prompt (Unless You Want to Die Alone)

Before we dive into the good stuff, let's talk about the answers that'll make you look like a complete tool.

If you're using any of these, stop it. Seriously.

Here are five examples of answers that'll have your matches running for the hills:

- ... you (Congrats, you've just told everyone you're desperate and probably a stalker)
- ... sex (Great, you've reduced yourself to a hormonal teenager)
- ... money (Nothing says "I have a personality" like being a walking ATM)
- ... myself (Narcissism isn't as attractive as you think it is)
- ... pizza (Unless you're a Ninja Turtle, find a more interesting obsession)
  These answers are about as appealing as a root canal. They're lazy, they're boring, and they scream "I have the personality of a stale cracker." You're on a dating app, not writing your shopping list. Act like it.

Alright, listen up. If you're going to go the cheeky route, you better commit to it. Half-assed cheekiness is about as attractive as a hangover on a Monday morning. The goal here is to make them laugh, not roll their eyes so hard they see their own brain.

## The Art of Balance: Humor, Personality, and Authenticity

Now, let's talk about the secret sauce that'll make your Hinge profile irresistible: balance. You want to strike the perfect chord between humor and sincerity, showcasing your personality without coming off as a try-hard or a one-dimensional joke machine.

Remember, dating apps like Hinge are tools in modern dating, but they're not the whole toolbox. Your profile should be a teaser trailer for the blockbuster that is you. Here's how to nail it:

1. **Mix it up**: Don't make every answer a joke. Sprinkle in some humor, add a dash of personal stories, and top it off with a hint of your life goals.
2. **Be authentic**: As tempting as it is to create a "perfect" profile, authenticity wins every time. Show your genuine personality traits. If you're a bit of a geek who goes crazy for Mario Kart tournaments, own it!
3. **Show, don't tell**: Instead of saying "I'm funny," demonstrate your sense of humor through your answers. Same goes for any other quality you want to highlight.
4. **Keep it fresh**: Update your answers occasionally. It keeps your profile interesting for repeat viewers and shows you're actively engaged.
5. **Use visuals**: If Hinge allows, add photos that complement your answers. Going crazy for travel? Add a picture from your latest adventure.
6. **Be specific**: "I go crazy for dogs" is okay, but "I go crazy for teaching my corgi new tricks" is better. It gives potential matches more to work with.
7. **Mind the length**: Aim for answers that are long enough to be interesting but short enough to leave them wanting more.
8. **Proofread**: Nothing kills the vibe faster than typos or grammatical errors. Give your profile a once-over before setting it live.
   Remember, dating coach extraordinaire Logan Ury says that your profile should be a conversation starter. Each answer should give potential matches something to ask about or comment on.
