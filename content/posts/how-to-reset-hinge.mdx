<TLDR>

### Before You Delete Hinge

- Hinge does not document a universal “algorithm reset” button or guaranteed new-account boost.
- Edit photos, prompts, preferences, and Dealbreakers when presentation or the candidate pool is the problem.
- Pause the account when you want a break without immediately deleting profile history.
- Download your data and review subscription billing before deletion.
- Deleting the app does not delete the account and does not cancel a store subscription.
- Do not recreate identities, phone numbers, devices, or payment methods to evade a ban.

</TLDR>

## What Kind of Reset Do You Need?

“Reset Hinge” can describe four different actions:

1. **Refresh the profile:** update photos and prompts.
2. **Change the pool:** review preferences, distance, and Dealbreakers.
3. **Take a break:** pause the account.
4. **Remove the account:** permanently delete it.

Deleting should be the last option because it removes profile content, chats, and member interactions under Hinge's privacy policy.

## Refresh the Existing Profile

Update the elements a real person uses to decide:

- replace old or unclear photos;
- reorder photos and prompts deliberately;
- make prompt answers specific and easy to comment on;
- review relationship intentions and profile fields;
- remove Dealbreakers that are preferences rather than requirements.

Hinge offers Prompt Feedback and Top Photo tools in supported accounts. Those can help with presentation, but Hinge does not promise that an edit creates a temporary visibility boost.

Change one major element at a time if you want to understand the result. Replacing everything while changing location and paying for a Boost produces a noisy comparison.

## Pause Instead of Delete

Use Hinge's pause control when you want to stop appearing to new people without permanently removing the account. The exact menu label can change, so follow **Settings** and the current pause option in your app.

Pausing is appropriate for travel, dating someone exclusively, burnout, or simply needing time. It avoids losing chats and profile history only to recreate the same account later.

## Download Your Data First

Hinge says active members can request an export in the app:

1. Open the Photo icon.
2. Go to Settings.
3. Tap **Download My Data**.
4. Select the country and confirm the email.

Exports can take up to 30 days and remain available for 48 hours after delivery. They do not include another member's personal information. Hinge's conversation export provides only your half of the chat.

Request the export before deletion if you want to preserve your own account history.

## Cancel or Review the Subscription

Subscription behavior depends on the payment route.

- App Store subscriptions must be managed through Apple.
- Google Play subscriptions must be managed through Google Play.
- Hinge payment subscriptions use Hinge/Stripe controls.

Deleting the app does not cancel any subscription. Hinge says deleting the account immediately cancels a Hinge-payment subscription but does not automatically cancel App Store or Google Play billing. Verify the result with the original billing platform.

## Delete the Hinge Account

Hinge's current deletion flow is:

1. Sign in to the Hinge app.
2. Tap the Photo icon.
3. Open the Gear icon for Account Settings.
4. Scroll to **Delete Account**.
5. Follow the prompts to confirm.

Uninstalling the app is not enough. Hinge says proper deletion removes profile content, photos, chat history, and member interactions according to its privacy policy.

## Does Recreating Hinge Reset the Algorithm?

Hinge does not publish a guarantee that deletion and recreation produces a particular recommendation reset or fixed new-user boost.

A new experience can differ because the profile, preferences, local pool, recent activity, and product version differ. That does not isolate deletion as the cause.

Hinge's automated-decision disclosure says recommendations can use age, gender, location, preferences, Dealbreakers, Likes, skips, matches, and some phone-number exchanges. It does not describe a user-accessible score that deletion reliably erases for better results.

## What If the Account Is Banned?

Use the Appeal option on Hinge's ban screen. Do not create new phone numbers, emails, devices, payment identities, photos, or accounts to bypass enforcement.

Hinge says appeals use the designated in-app process and may require government-issued ID. A reset guide should not undermine those safety controls.

## FAQ

### Can I reset Hinge without deleting it?

You can edit the profile, change preferences, and pause the account. Hinge does not document a separate algorithm-reset control.

### Does deleting the Hinge app delete the account?

No. Use **Settings → Delete Account** and confirm.

### Does deleting Hinge cancel a subscription?

Not every subscription. Store purchases must be canceled through Apple or Google. Hinge says account deletion cancels Hinge-payment subscriptions immediately.

### Is there a guaranteed new-user boost?

Hinge does not document a universal fixed boost period in the official sources reviewed here.

### How do I reset a banned Hinge account?

Use the in-app appeal. Do not attempt identity or device evasion.
