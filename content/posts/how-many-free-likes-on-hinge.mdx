<TLDR>

### The Current Answer

Hinge says free members can send a **limited number of Likes per day**, but its current English subscription help pages do not promise one universal count for every account. The app is the source of truth for your remaining allowance. Hinge+ and HingeX include unlimited Likes.

</TLDR>

## Why We Are Not Publishing a Fixed Number

Many older guides state that every free Hinge account receives a specific daily count. Hinge's current help pages use “limited” rather than guaranteeing one number across markets, accounts, and product tests.

If your app shows an allowance or limit message, trust that current interface. A fixed article number can become wrong without warning.

The reset timing can likewise be account- and product-dependent. Hinge tells you when more Likes become available; it does not document a universal clock time on the current subscription page.

## What Counts as a Like?

In Discover, tap the heart on a specific photo or prompt and optionally add a comment. If the other person accepts, you match.

Standouts uses Roses rather than ordinary Likes. Hinge provides one free Rose per week, and that separate allowance does not change the free daily Like limit.

## What Do Hinge+ and HingeX Change?

Both subscriptions include unlimited Likes. They also provide the ability to see all incoming Likes at once and use Advanced Preferences.

HingeX adds Enhanced Recommendations, Skip the Line, and Priority Likes. If the only problem is reaching the daily Like limit, compare Hinge+ before paying for the additional priority features.

## Should You Use Every Free Like?

No. A daily allowance is a ceiling, not a target.

Like people you would genuinely want to meet. Sending a Like only to exhaust the allowance creates more irrelevant matches and gives the recommendation system noisier preference information.

Hinge says Likes and skips are among the behaviors used by its matching system. It does not document a magic selectivity percentage or a fixed punishment for using all available Likes.

## Do Comments Use Extra Likes?

Adding a comment is part of sending the Like; it is not described as a separate Like charge. The comment should reference the exact photo or prompt and give the recipient an easy response path.

## What If You Hit the Limit?

Wait for the in-app allowance to refresh, review existing matches, or consider a subscription if the limit repeatedly prevents genuine Likes.

Hinge also uses Your Turn Limits: accounts with too many people waiting for a response may need to reply or close conversations before sending or receiving new Likes. That is different from the ordinary free daily allowance and applies to subscribers too.

## FAQ

### How many Likes does free Hinge give you?

Hinge confirms a limited daily amount but does not publish one universal count in its current subscription pages. Check the allowance shown in your app.

### When do Hinge Likes reset?

Use the reset information shown in the app. Hinge's current subscription page does not guarantee one clock time for every account.

### Are Likes unlimited with Hinge+?

Yes. Both Hinge+ and HingeX include unlimited Likes.

### Are Roses part of the daily Like limit?

Roses have a separate balance. Hinge provides one free Rose each week, and Standouts uses Roses.

### Can a subscriber still be blocked from sending Likes?

Yes, through Your Turn Limits. A member with too many conversations awaiting their response may need to reply or close chats before making new connections.
