<TLDR>

### Choose the Tier by the Job

- **Plus:** unlimited Likes, Rewinds, Passport, reduced ads, and privacy controls, subject to current plan details.
- **Gold:** Plus features plus Likes You, Top Picks, weekly Super Likes, and an eligible monthly Boost.
- **Platinum:** Gold features plus First Impressions, Priority Likes, and recent sent-Like history in supported markets.
- Prices and even some included benefits vary by market, platform, plan length, and product tests.
- No tier guarantees that another person will see or accept a Like.

</TLDR>

## Tinder's Three Main Subscription Tiers

Tinder's official subscription guide currently lists Tinder Plus, Tinder Gold, and Tinder Platinum. The tiers build on one another.

| Feature | Plus | Gold | Platinum |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Unlimited daily Likes | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Rewinds and Passport | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Incognito and ad controls | Yes, subject to tests | Yes | Yes |
| See who Likes You | No | Yes | Yes |
| Top Picks | No | Yes | Yes |
| Weekly Super Likes | No | Yes | Yes |
| Eligible monthly Boost | No | Often, with plan/test caveats | Often, with plan/test caveats |
| First Impressions | No | No | Yes |
| Priority Likes | No | No | Yes |
| Sent Likes from recent days | No | No | In supported markets |

Tinder explicitly notes that advertisements, Rewinds, Top Picks, sent-Like history, and monthly Boost availability can differ during tests. Read the checkout details for the exact account instead of assuming the table is a contractual promise.

## Tinder Plus: Remove Usage Friction

Plus is mainly useful when the free-product limits or travel workflow are the constraint:

- you regularly run out of Likes;
- you want Passport before a trip;
- you frequently need Rewind;
- or Incognito is important for privacy.

Unlimited Likes are not a reason to Like everyone. Tinder's recommendation system and other users still make individual decisions, and quantity does not turn an unsuitable profile into a match.

## Tinder Gold: Review Incoming Demand

Gold's defining feature is **Likes You**, which lets you see people who have already Liked the profile before choosing Like or Nope. It also adds Top Picks and may include Super Likes and a monthly Boost under the current plan.

Gold can save time when there is a meaningful incoming-Likes queue. It has less value when that queue is empty or the user prefers evaluating Discover normally.

## Tinder Platinum: Improve Placement and Add Context

Platinum adds Priority Likes and First Impressions. Priority Likes are shown faster than Likes from non-subscribers, but Tinder explicitly says they are not guaranteed to be seen because Discovery preferences and other factors still apply.

First Impressions allow a short message to be sent before matching. They are limited and subject to eligibility. A message can add context to a Like; it cannot bypass the recipient's decision.

## How Much Does Tinder Cost?

There is no reliable universal price table. Tinder's displayed price can vary by purchase platform, region, currency, plan duration, tax, and active offer. The price and renewal terms on the confirmation screen are the current terms for that purchase.

Compare total cost, not only a weekly or monthly headline. A longer plan may reduce the effective monthly price while increasing the amount committed upfront.

## A Simple Decision Framework

1. Use the free app long enough to identify the bottleneck.
2. Name the single feature that addresses it.
3. Check whether that feature is included in the exact offer shown.
4. Choose the shortest sensible test period.
5. Set a cancellation reminder before renewal.
6. Evaluate outcomes that matter—useful matches and conversations—not only Likes sent.

If the problem is profile clarity, intent, or photo selection, fix that first. Those improvements affect every impression across free and paid distribution.

## Canceling, Downgrading, and Restoring

Cancel through the billing provider used for purchase: Apple, Google Play, Tinder's direct payment account, or a third-party merchant. Deleting the app or Tinder account does not cancel billing.

Downgrades may require canceling, waiting for the current term to expire, and subscribing to the lower tier. If an active purchase is missing after reinstalling or changing devices, use **Restore Purchase** rather than paying again.

## FAQ

### Which Tinder tier is best?

There is no universal best tier. Plus addresses usage limits and travel, Gold adds incoming-Like visibility, and Platinum adds placement and pre-match messaging features.

### Does Platinum guarantee more matches?

No. Tinder says even Priority Likes are not guaranteed to be seen.

### Does every Gold plan include a monthly Boost?

No. Weekly plans do not, and Tinder says some longer Gold plans may omit it during tests.

### Does deleting Tinder cancel the subscription?

No. Cancel with the billing provider.
