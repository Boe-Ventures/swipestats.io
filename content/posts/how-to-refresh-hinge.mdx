<TLDR>

### Refresh Hinge in This Order

1. Check Wi-Fi or mobile data.
2. Confirm you used the original login method.
3. Log out and back in to re-sync with Hinge's servers.
4. Update the app and restart the device.
5. Reinstall the app if needed; do **not** tap Delete Account.
6. Contact Hinge support with the time, app version, OS version, screenshots, and steps already tried.

</TLDR>

## Refresh Missing Matches or Stale Data

Hinge says missing matches are usually temporary while the app re-syncs.

Start with:

- a strong Wi-Fi or mobile-data connection;
- logging out and back in;
- waiting briefly for a large match list to load;
- restarting the device;
- updating Hinge to the latest version.

Use the same sign-in method originally used—phone, Google, or Apple.

## Reinstall Hinge Without Deleting the Profile

Removing and reinstalling the app can refresh local software and install the latest version. Hinge says the profile remains intact as long as you do not choose **Delete Account** inside Hinge.

Deleting the app is not the same as deleting the account. It also does not pause or cancel a subscription.

## Refresh the Profile Content

If “refresh” means improving the profile rather than fixing a technical error:

- update old photos;
- rewrite generic prompts with specific details;
- review relationship intentions and profile fields;
- adjust location manually after moving or travel;
- review distance and Dealbreakers.

Hinge offers Top Photo and Prompt Feedback in supported accounts. Hinge does not document a guaranteed visibility boost from changing one photo or prompt.

## Pause Instead of Delete

To take a break:

1. Tap the Photo icon.
2. Open the Gear icon.
3. Turn on **Pause**.

Pausing prevents the profile from being shown to new people while preserving existing chats. Previously sent Likes can still reach recipients. Hinge says pausing does not pause a subscription, and Top Photo can continue testing and reordering photos.

## When to Contact Support

If the basic steps fail, include:

- a clear description;
- the date and time;
- account email or phone information;
- OS and OS version;
- Hinge app version;
- screenshots or a screen recording;
- troubleshooting already attempted.

Hinge warns that fake support phone numbers exist. Use the official Help Center request form; legitimate support does not ask to connect remotely to your computer.

## What Not to Do

- Do not delete the account to fix a temporary sync issue.
- Do not repeatedly create new accounts.
- Do not use a new phone number, device, payment method, or identity to evade a restriction.
- Do not pay a third-party “support” number.
- Do not assume a quiet match week proves a technical or algorithmic problem.

## FAQ

### How do I force Hinge to refresh matches?

Check the connection, log out and back in, update the app, restart the device, and wait for the server sync.

### Will reinstalling Hinge delete matches?

Hinge says the profile remains intact if you only remove the app and do not choose Delete Account.

### Does changing a prompt boost visibility?

Hinge does not document a guaranteed visibility boost from a profile edit.

### Can I refresh Hinge by pausing?

Pause is for hiding the profile from new people while keeping existing chats. It is not documented as an algorithm reset.
