<TLDR>

### The Principle

Text this woman based on what she has said and how the two of you relate—not on a theory about all women.

> You mentioned the night market and made it sound excellent. Want to go together Friday?

</TLDR>

## Start With Context

- “I enjoyed meeting you at Maya's party. How did the rest of the night go?”
- “Your profile says you restore old furniture. What project taught you the most?”
- “You recommended that film, and I watched it. I have questions.”
- “How did the interview go?”
- “I liked talking with you and wanted to continue.”

Context shows why you are messaging and gives her an answerable topic.

## Do Not Conduct an Interview

Ask one question and share something too:

> What got you into running? I signed up for my first 10K and currently regret every hill.

Disclosure helps create reciprocity. Endless questions can feel like you are collecting data rather than connecting.

## Flirt Clearly

- “You looked very good tonight.”
- “I like the way your mind works.”
- “I am enjoying getting to know you.”
- “You are a very pleasant distraction.”
- “I would like to kiss you next time. How does that sound?”

The last example asks rather than assumes. Consent is specific; attraction does not entitle anyone to sexual content or contact.

## Ask for a Date

> I am enjoying this. Want to get coffee at North Street Saturday afternoon?

Include a public activity and a time window. If she cannot make it but wants to meet, she can suggest another option.

Avoid “We should hang sometime” when you actually mean a date. Ambiguity shifts the work of defining your intention onto her.

## Reply Timing

There is no correct delay. Reply when you have attention to give, and say when you need to leave:

> I am heading into work, but I want to hear the rest later.

Do not punish a slow reply with an even slower one, monitor online status, or send repeated checks. One delay reveals very little.

## Compliments

Specific compliments can create conversation:

- “Your answer about community work was thoughtful.”
- “That jacket is excellent. Vintage?”
- “You explain that subject with contagious enthusiasm.”
- “Your laugh made the date better.”

Do not make every message about her body. Attraction can include style, humor, judgment, kindness, and shared energy.

## If She Is Not Interested

A no, an unmatch, or a request to stop is complete information. Respond respectfully and stop contact.

Do not locate her on another platform after an unmatch. Hinge's own community guidance says to respect that choice rather than finding the person on social media.

## Avoid “Female Psychology” Rules

Claims such as “women always test men,” “never double text,” or “make her chase” turn an individual into a stereotype and encourage manipulation.

Ask about her preferences. Observe the mutual pattern. State your own needs. If those do not fit, the solution is compatibility—not a better trick.

## FAQ

### How do I keep her interested over text?

You cannot control interest. You can be attentive, truthful, reciprocal, and willing to make a real plan.

### Should I text every day?

Only if the rhythm feels mutual. Ask rather than enforce a rule.

### Is double texting bad?

One relevant follow-up can be fine. Repeated messages after no reply create pressure.

### How do I know if she likes me?

Look at the broader reciprocal pattern and ask directly when clarity matters.
