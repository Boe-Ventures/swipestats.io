<TLDR>

### Hinge Boost in 30 Seconds

- A Hinge Boost increases profile visibility for **one hour**. A Superboost lasts **24 hours**.
- Activation is immediate when you tap the Boost button, so prepare the profile and timing before using it.
- Hinge shows a post-Boost summary and marks Likes attributed to the Boost with a lightning icon.
- Other members are not told that the profile is being Boosted.
- More visibility is not the same as more matches. A Boost is easiest to evaluate when the profile already converts some ordinary exposure into Likes.

</TLDR>

## What Does a Hinge Boost Do?

Hinge says Boost places the profile more prominently and makes it visible to more potential matches for one hour. Superboost extends that period to 24 hours.

The feature changes distribution. It does not change photos, prompts, preferences, or the recipient's decision. If a profile is unclear, Boost can expose the same problem to more people.

Hinge does not notify other members that a profile is Boosted. When the period ends, the app shows a summary of impact, and Likes directly attributed to Boost display a lightning icon.

## How to Buy and Activate Hinge Boost

Hinge's current flow starts from the Likes You screen:

1. Open **Likes You**.
2. Tap the Boost button to purchase or view available Boosts.
3. If a Boost is already available, tapping the button activates it immediately.
4. Watch the countdown to see the remaining period.

Because activation is immediate, do not tap through casually if you intend to save it for a different time.

## Boost vs. Superboost vs. HingeX

| Product | Distribution treatment |
| --- | --- |
| Boost | Increased visibility for one hour |
| Superboost | Increased visibility for 24 hours |
| HingeX Skip the Line | Ongoing priority described as an always-on Boost |

These are different purchasing models for added visibility. HingeX also includes Hinge+ benefits, Enhanced Recommendations, and Priority Likes, while an individual Boost is a time-limited consumable.

## When a Boost May Be Worth Testing

A Boost is a more useful experiment when:

- the profile already receives some incoming Likes;
- the local pool is large and active;
- photos and prompts are complete and current;
- preferences are broad enough to create an eligible audience;
- you can remain available to review and respond after activation.

It is less informative when the account is brand new, the profile changes at the same time, the local pool is very small, or the main goal is to fix missing matches without understanding the baseline.

## How to Run a Cleaner Boost Test

1. **Record normal performance.** Use several comparable days, not one unusually active evening.
2. **Keep the profile stable.** Do not replace photos during the test.
3. **Keep preferences and location stable.** Those change the eligible pool.
4. **Record the attributed Likes.** Use Hinge's lightning marker and result summary.
5. **Compare quality as well as count.** More Likes outside your preferences may not create value.
6. **Repeat only if the first result justifies the cost.** One small test is better than buying a large pack on hope.

Hinge's attribution marker is useful product evidence. It still does not tell you how many impressions occurred, which profiles would have seen you anyway, or whether the resulting match becomes a date.

## What Time Should You Use Hinge Boost?

Hinge does not publish one globally optimal hour in its current Boost help page. Local usage varies by city, time zone, weekday, season, and audience.

Use the feature when your intended pool is plausibly active and when you can respond afterward. Treat any “best hour” from another market as a hypothesis, not a guarantee.

## How Much Does Hinge Boost Cost?

Hinge does not maintain a universal public price in its help documentation. The offer can vary by market, platform, package, and promotion. Check the total shown in the app before confirming.

Compare the full package price and number of Boosts rather than only an advertised per-item equivalent. Also compare the cost with HingeX if ongoing Skip the Line is the actual feature you want.

## Can SwipeStats Measure a Boost Lift?

An export may show Likes, matches, and activity, but it does not necessarily contain every profile impression or a durable Boost label for each event. SwipeStats therefore cannot promise a universal multiplier from uploaded data.

Use Hinge's in-app attributed Like marker for the immediate test, and use export analytics for broader before-and-after context. Do not claim the Boost caused every difference between two weeks.

## FAQ

### How long does a Hinge Boost last?

A standard Boost lasts one hour. A Superboost lasts 24 hours.

### Can other people see that I used a Boost?

Hinge says other members are not notified.

### Does Hinge Boost guarantee Likes or matches?

No. It increases visibility. Recipients still decide whether to Like or match.

### Does HingeX include Boost?

HingeX includes Skip the Line, which Hinge describes as an always-on Boost. It is not the same activation flow as a one-hour purchased Boost.

### Is Hinge Boost worth it?

It can be worth one controlled test for an already-working profile in an active market. It is poor evidence when several other variables change at the same time.
