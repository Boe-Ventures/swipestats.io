<TLDR>

### The Main Hinge Icons

- **H:** Discover feed
- **Star:** Standouts
- **Heart:** Likes You
- **Chat bubble:** Matches and conversations
- **Photo/profile icon:** Profile hub and settings
- **Heart on a photo or prompt:** Send a Like
- **X:** Skip
- **Rose:** Send higher-placement interest

</TLDR>

## Bottom Navigation

Hinge's exact visual design can change by app version, but the current help pages describe these core destinations.

### H: Discover

Discover is the main recommendation feed. Tap the Heart beside a specific photo or prompt to prepare a Like, or tap X to Skip. The undo arrow can restore only the most recent Skip when it is available.

### Star: Standouts

Standouts is a separate feed of profiles receiving attention that meet your preferences. Interest from this feed is sent with a Rose rather than an ordinary Like.

### Heart: Likes You

Incoming Likes appear here. A numerical badge can indicate new activity. Free users review incoming profiles one at a time, while subscription features can change how the queue is viewed.

### Chat Bubble: Matches

Mutual Likes become Matches. This is where conversations, unread activity, Hidden chats, and chat-specific controls appear.

### Profile Photo

The far-right profile area opens editing, preferences, settings, notifications, subscriptions, Pause, and account controls.

## Discover Actions

| Icon or control | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Heart | Like the selected photo or prompt |
| Comment field | Add a message to the Like before sending |
| X | Skip the current profile |
| Undo arrow | Restore only the most recent eligible Skip |
| Rose | Send interest with top placement in Likes You |
| Three dots | Open Remove or Report options |

A Rose improves placement; it does not guarantee that the recipient sees or accepts the profile.

## Profile Badges

### Selfie Verified

The verification badge means a video-selfie check matched a live face with profile photos. Hinge says it is not proof of a person's name, background, intentions, or safety.

### Signals

A Signals badge reflects patterns of recent thoughtful participation and follow-through inside Hinge. Eligibility is recalculated and the badge can appear or disappear. Hinge says it cannot be purchased and does not guarantee responsiveness, compatibility, character, or relationship outcomes.

### New Here

New Here identifies a profile that recently joined, when the feature is available. Treat it as account-age context, not a trust or quality score.

Feature badges can vary by market, test, and subscription. Do not assume an absent badge means something is wrong with the account.

## Match and Chat Controls

Inside a conversation, the three-dot menu can include:

- **Enable or Disable Notifications** for that chat;
- **Hide** or **Unhide** the conversation;
- **Unmatch**, which permanently removes the connection and chat;
- **Report**, for a safety or policy issue;
- **We Met**, where available, for private date feedback.

Matches with no activity for 14 days can move into Hidden automatically. A new message returns a hidden conversation to the top.

## “Your Turn” and Limits

Hinge uses Your Turn state to identify conversations awaiting your response. If too many people are waiting, Your Turn Limits can prevent new Like activity until you reply to or close existing conversations.

The other person is not notified that you hit the limit. The indicator describes product state; it does not reveal how anyone feels about the conversation.

## Why Your Icons May Look Different

Hinge tests features and updates the interface. Differences can result from:

- iOS vs. Android;
- app version;
- region;
- free vs. subscribed account;
- feature rollout or test eligibility.

Update the app and use the current label shown beside an icon. An old screenshot is weaker evidence than the live control and official help page.

## FAQ

### What does the X mean?

It skips the current Discover profile. It is unrelated to the HingeX subscription.

### What does the Rose mean?

It sends a higher-placement Like and is required in Standouts.

### Does the verified badge mean the person is safe?

No. Hinge explicitly says Selfie Verification is not a safety guarantee.

### What does Signals mean?

It reflects recent in-app participation patterns. Hinge says it does not guarantee responsiveness or outcomes.
