<TLDR>

Hinge's current purchase documentation does not list a dedicated “Hinge gift card” or a way to buy Hinge+ or HingeX directly for another account.

Apple or Google Play credit may be usable when the recipient subscribes through that same store, but region, account, balance, tax, and payment rules apply. Hinge also offers direct Hinge/Stripe billing to some iOS users, where app-store credit would not fund the purchase.

</TLDR>

## How Hinge Billing Currently Works

Hinge says iOS users may see two purchase routes:

- **Hinge payment powered by Stripe**; or
- **Apple App Store billing**.

Android purchases are processed through Google Play. Available plans, prices, and payment options appear inside the recipient's app and can vary.

Because the billing account belongs to the dater, you should not ask for their password, verification code, or payment login to make the purchase yourself.

## Can You Give Apple or Google Play Credit?

Potentially, but treat it as general store credit rather than a guaranteed Hinge subscription.

Check before buying:

1. Which phone and store account does the recipient use?
2. Is the card valid in the same country or region as that account?
3. Does the recipient plan to use store billing rather than Hinge/Stripe?
4. Is the balance enough for the in-app total, including any applicable tax?
5. Is the store credit permitted for subscriptions under the store's current rules?

Do not quote an old Hinge price when choosing the amount. Ask the recipient to view the current packages inside the app.

## A Lower-Risk Gift

Cash, a general-purpose permitted payment gift, or a date-night experience avoids tying the recipient to a particular subscription or renewal. You can also offer help selecting photos—only if they want it—without taking over the profile.

Dating subscriptions can be personal. Make sure the gift will feel supportive rather than like criticism of somebody's dating life.

## Important Renewal Detail

Hinge subscriptions auto-renew unless canceled. A prepaid balance does not necessarily prevent a future charge to another payment method. The account owner should review the renewal date and cancel through the original billing platform if they do not want renewal.

Deleting the Hinge app does not cancel App Store, Google Play, or Hinge/Stripe billing.

## Avoid Gift-Card Scams

Do not buy a card for an online match who claims it is required to verify an account, travel to meet you, restore service, or solve an emergency. Hinge does not require one dater to fund another person's account.
