<TLDR>

- Facebook Dating is accessed from an existing Facebook account in the Android or iPhone app.
- You create a separate Dating profile.
- You can send Likes and messages to people you are interested in; when interest is mutual, you match and can chat in Dating.
- Dating activity does not appear in the main Facebook Feed, and Dating conversations are separate from Messenger.
- Availability depends on age, location, account status, and rollout.

</TLDR>

## Who Can Use It?

Meta says Facebook Dating requires an adult Facebook account in good standing and is not available to everyone or in every location. The app may request location services and, in some cases, age or profile verification.

Use the Facebook mobile app's menu to look for **Dating**. A page found through desktop search is not evidence that the product is available to your account.

## Creating the Dating Profile

The setup flow asks questions and lets you review the Dating profile before confirming it. This profile is distinct from the main Facebook profile.

Meta says current Facebook friends and people outside Dating cannot see the Dating profile, and Dating activity does not appear in the main Feed. Do not confuse that separation with total anonymity: other people using Dating can see the profile, and anything shared with a match can be copied.

## Likes, Messages, and Matches

Facebook's help center says you can send Likes and messages to people you are interested in. Once someone Likes you back, you match and can chat within Dating.

Dating conversations stay separate from Facebook Messenger. Keep early communication in the dating product, avoid sending money, and do not disclose home, work, financial, or identity details prematurely.

## Suggested Matches and Preferences

Meta offers preference settings and documents suggestions based on information and activity associated with Dating. Location services support distance-based suggestions. Features such as friends-of-friends controls, Instagram connections, Match Anywhere, and Secret Crush can depend on the account or region.

Treat preferences as inputs, not guarantees. The exact ordering system is not a promise that every suggestion meets every preference.

## What Happens to Your Facebook Friends?

Facebook Dating is designed not to show current Facebook friends as ordinary Dating suggestions, while optional features can create other paths. Review the current friends-of-friends and Secret Crush settings rather than assuming a universal default.

## Deleting Facebook Dating

You can delete the Dating profile without deleting the Facebook account. Deleting the Facebook account also deletes the Dating profile. Meta says that after deleting Dating, you cannot create a new Dating profile for seven days.

## Safety

Meta states that it does not conduct criminal background checks or sex-offender registry screening for every dater. Meet in public, arrange your own transportation, tell a trusted person your plan, protect identifying information, and report suspicious requests.
