### The 3-Category Framework That Actually Works

This comes from sex and dating coach Eden Lee Mitelman, and it's the best framework I've found because it's dead simple and covers every dirty talk scenario you'll ever encounter.

Three categories. That's it. You don't need a PhD.

2. **Anticipation**: "I can't wait to..." / "I've been thinking about you all day." / "Do you know what I'm going to do when I see you?" This works over text AND in person. It builds tension. 3. **Demands**: "Keep doing that." "I want you to..." "Come here." These aren't aggressive commands. They're invitations with urgency. The difference between a demand and a creepy order is consent and context. If you're both already in the moment, "don't stop" is not aggressive. It's communication.

That's the whole framework. Every dirty talk phrase you'll ever need falls into one of these three buckets. Compliment, anticipate, or demand. Mix and match. You're welcome.

## FAQ

### How do I start if I've never done dirty talk before?

Texts. Always start with texts. Lower stakes, more time to think, easier to course-correct if something lands weird. Use the warm-tier phrases from the list above. Send one. See how they respond. If they match your energy, go a step further next time. If they change the subject, respect it and try again another time. You're not defusing a bomb. You're flirting. Relax.

### How to talk dirty without being creepy?

Consent and escalation. Those are your two magic words. Start mild, read the response, only escalate if they're clearly into it. "Creepy" almost always comes from skipping steps. From saying something explicit before the other person has given any signal they want that. Match their energy. If they send you something suggestive, you can send something suggestive back. If they send you "haha yeah work was fine," do NOT respond with your fantasies. Context. Always context.

### How to talk dirty in a long distance relationship?

Sexting and voice notes become your primary love language. Long distance couples who maintain sexual communication report higher relationship satisfaction. Schedule it if you have to. "FaceTime at 10pm, wear something nice" builds anticipation all day. Use the anticipation category from the framework aggressively. "I've been thinking about what I'm going to do when I finally see you" hits ten times harder when there's actual distance and time between you. The longing does half the work.

### What if I feel ridiculous saying this stuff?

Everyone does at first. That's why practicing alone works. Say the phrases out loud until they stop making you cringe. Start with easy ones: "that feels good," "I love when you do that," "you're so hot." These are training-wheels dirty talk. Nobody in history has ever felt ridiculous saying "you feel amazing" to someone they're sleeping with. Build from there. And remember, your partner is not grading you. They're turned on by the effort, the vulnerability, and the fact that you're actually communicating instead of lying there in silence like a human mannequin.
