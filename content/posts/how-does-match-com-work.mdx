<TLDR>

Match lets adults build a profile, set preferences, browse or receive suggested profiles, Like people, and form mutual matches. Match's current help center says free members can chat with every mutual match. Optional subscriptions add features, and prices should be checked on the account's purchase screen.

</TLDR>

## Create a Profile and Preferences

The profile supplies information about you; preference settings describe who you hope to meet. Fill in specific, current details and use recent photos that clearly show you.

Preferences affect discovery, but overly narrow settings can produce fewer results. Match's Mutual Search uses both sides' stated criteria to identify people who may fit each other.

## Discover People

Match supports search and recommendation surfaces rather than only a single swipe queue. Available filters and labels can vary by platform and subscription.

Use filters to remove true incompatibilities, not to construct an imaginary perfect person. A profile is still a limited self-description, and a recommendation is not a safety screening or compatibility guarantee.

## Likes, Matches, and Messages

Like a profile to express interest. When both people Like each other, it becomes a mutual match.

Match's current messaging documentation says all free members can chat with every match. Either person can begin a conversation after mutual interest. It also warns that messages cannot be unsent or recalled, so review the recipient and text before sending.

Match may show messages or Likes from people who are not mutual matches in different areas of the interface, while access and response options can depend on account and plan. Use the current in-product explanation when a feature is gated.

## Paid Plans

Subscriptions can add discovery, visibility, or communication features, and Match currently has multiple plan tiers. Features, promotions, renewal periods, and prices change, so do not rely on an old article's monthly figure.

Before subscribing, capture:

- the total billed today;
- the length of the initial term;
- the renewal amount and date;
- the cancellation route;
- which benefits are actually included for your account.

Free trials can require payment information and may renew unless canceled before the trial ends.

## Is Match Worth It?

Start with the free account and inspect the local pool. A paid feature cannot create compatible people nearby, improve unclear photos, or guarantee replies. Consider paying only when a specific locked feature addresses a problem you can name.

Judge the product over a defined period using outcomes you control: profiles reviewed thoughtfully, relevant Likes sent, reciprocal conversations, and safe dates—not merely notifications.

## Safety

Keep identifying and financial information private, never send money to a match, use in-product reporting, and meet in public with your own transportation. Consent is ongoing online and offline.
